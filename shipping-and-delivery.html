Frequently Asked Questions

Note: SkipEat is not responsible for food quality, allergies, or restaurant service failures (restaurants are).

1. How to place an order?

Open the menu in the SkipEat app, select dishes, and complete the payment. The order goes directly to the restaurant.

2. Can I cancel after ordering?

Yes, only before the restaurant starts preparation. After that, cancellation may not be possible.

3. What if the restaurant delays my order?

You’ll be notified in case of delays. If it’s significant, you can raise a support request for resolution/refund.

4. How do I get a refund?

Refunds apply if cancellation happens before preparation or if verified issues are reported within 24 hours.

5. Can I modify my order after placing it?

Only before preparation starts.

6. Do I need to pay extra for using SkipEat?

No, you only pay the restaurant’s price (plus applicable taxes).

7. How do I know if my order is confirmed?

You’ll receive instant confirmation in the app and via notification.

8. What if I am late to pick up my order?

Notify the restaurant via the app. They may keep food ready for a limited time, but freshness is not guaranteed.

9. Does SkipEat work 24/7?

SkipEat is available 24/7, but restaurant availability depends on their working hours.
