Cancellation & Refund Policy

Order Cancellation

Orders can only be cancelled before the restaurant starts preparing the order. Once preparation begins, cancellation may not be possible.

Refunds

  • If cancellation is approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • If issues with food quality, late service, or incorrect orders occur, report within 24 hours via the app for resolution

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after preparation begins
  • Delays caused by customer unavailability at restaurant dine-in time
